About Squaredle Solver
This is a solver for the popular word-finding game Squaredle.
Please note that the results generated may not be an exact match with Squaredle's word list, but should cover almost all the required words.

This site is in no way affiliated with Squaredle.
IMPORTANT: Use of this solver to artificially gain a perfect score in a short time and catapult to the top of the leaderboard is strongly discouraged, and can lead to your account being blocked from Squaredle's leaderboard.
